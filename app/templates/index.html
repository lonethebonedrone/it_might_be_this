Our Mission

Welcome to our project, a website to help make navigating Oakland's homeless shelters, programs, and support systems more easier. We hope this website is helpful to those in need or for those who want to volunteer at local homeless shelters.

What is homelessness?

Homelessness is the condition of not having stable, safe, and adequate housing, often due to a combination of poverty, lack of affordable housing, mental health issues, and systemic inequality. It affects individuals, families, and communities, with people living on the streets, in shelters, or in temporary accommodations. Addressing homelessness requires coordinated efforts in housing, healthcare, employment, and social services.

What are the effects of homelessness on teens?

30% of the homeless population are families with children, with 6% of children being unaccompanied. The Bay Area's environment makes it hard to find homeless shelters, with the lack of affordable housing being the main problem to homelessness in the Bay Area.
