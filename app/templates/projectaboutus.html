About the Website


We created this website in hopes to spread awareness of homelessness and to also help people find out and understand the different homeless shelters that the city of Oakland provides. We chose to create a website on this issue because it's more common to see someone houseless in the Bay Area than a lot of other places, and therefore it has impacted us and our feelings as we've all seen it throughout our lives.
