Featured Oakland Homeless Shelters

Covenant House
200 Harrison Street, Oakland, California

A non-profit homeless shelter aimed towards rehabilitating youth homeless. CHC is all-inclusive and provides plenty of programs for young Californians to gain independence after homelessness.

CityTeam
722 Washington Street, Oakland, California

A community-oriented homeless shelter aiming to restoring lives with programs providing food, shelter, clothing, etc. Spreads Christianity to those who need love and long-lasting solutions.

Cob on Wood
Wood St and 24th, Oakland, California

A cob-based commons space, serving the wood street residents of West Oakland. Helps create houses and shelter for the homeless, giving them a place to stay rather than on the streets.

A Friendly Manor
2298 San Pablo Ave, Oakland, California

A safe homeless shelter dedicated for homeless women, and they can drop in anytime. Works to help homeless women take the "next step" towards a better life.
Alameda County Health Care for the Homeless
384 14th Street, Oakland, California

Provides to anyone in Alameda County, where all health services are provided without charge to those who cannot pay. A federally-funded homeless shelter, which gives heatlh services to the homeless.

Serenity House
2363A San Pablo Ave, Oakland, California
Helps homeless mothers get back on track from substance abuse and domestic violence. Includes programs such as a 16-week journey to learn how to handle money and have enough to afford a home for themselves.
